Travel around Mallorca without carrying your luggage

A holiday starts better without dragging bags through the airport and car park. Here is how to move around Mallorca hands-free from start to finish.

Luggage delivery from the airport to your hotel

As soon as you land, we collect your luggage and take it straight to your hotel or villa. You go and enjoy, with no queues or full boots.

Storage for the hours without a room

Early check-out or arrival before check-in? Store your bags by the hour and make the most of that free time at the beach or in the centre.

Change hotels without carrying anything

If you tour the island or the Tramuntana, we move your luggage from one hotel to the next. You travel light and your things wait for you on arrival.
